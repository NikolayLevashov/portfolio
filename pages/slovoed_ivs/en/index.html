Slovoed for Business
  • Do you represent an international company and need to establish effective communication between employees around the world?
  • Are you running a private translation agency?
  • Or do you head a transcontinental network of hotels and tourist accommodations?
  • Or perhaps a travel agency?
  • Whatever the case, a reliable and user-friendly digital dictionary is the key to success in language acquisition and establishing successful business communication in a foreign language.

The Slovoed Corporate Program offers flexible licensing options and the most trusted dictionaries for 40+ languages from the world’s most outstanding publishing houses, such as Cambridge University Press, Oxford University Press, PONS, Duden, Langenscheidt, Larousse Editorial, LeRobert. We also provide specialized expert dictionaries in the fields of law, medicine, finance, management, etc.

All products are cross-platfrom and available for the most popular mobile and desktop platforms: iOS, Android, Microsoft Windows and Mac OS X.

Individual offers, reasonable pricing, qualified support and implementation service for both state-owned and non-commercial organizations and much more!

Slovoed for Education

Mobile devices and apps are becoming more and more popular among young, well-educated people, as they provide so much convenience and productivity on the fly. Learning languages is generally a very complicated and time-consuming process, but Slovoed has created tools which make studies much easier and quicker, such as morphology tables and search hints, additional grammar rules and tests, flash card quizzes and the Slovoed spelling game.

Experience all digital advantages of the dictionary content from the world’s most authoritative linguistic experts!

The Slovoed Academic Program offers extended trials, special purchase conditions and qualified support service for all levels of educational institutions, from schools and colleges to universities and more.
